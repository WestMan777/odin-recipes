Brownie Recipe

Here is a recipe for Brownies. Bellow are the recipes ingredient list as well as the directions for how to make this recipe bellow
Ingredients
Brownies:
  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
Frosting:
  • Butter
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Honey
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Confectioners' Sugar
Directions
Make the Brownies:
  1. Melt the butter on the stove.
  2. Remove from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients.
  3. Beat in the dry ingredients.
  4. Spread the batter in the pan and bake.
Frost the Brownies:
  1. Stir the frosting ingredients together until well-combined.
  2. Frost the brownies while they are still warm.
  3. Cut into squares when cool.
